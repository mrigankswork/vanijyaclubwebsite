About The Club

Welcome to the Vanijya, the commerce club of Delhi Public School, Noida Vanijya club aims at building self confidence and inculcating entrepreneurial spirit among the students of classes IX to XII. It provides a plethora of opportunities to participate in intra and inter school events like Ad Mad Business Plan. Mock Stock, Logo Designing and the list is endless. The Club is instrumental in encouraging and motivating students to understand practical concepts of business and aims to develop entrepreneurial outlook and organisational skills in them.
